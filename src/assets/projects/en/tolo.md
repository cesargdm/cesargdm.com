# TOLO

[TOLO](https://tolo.cafe) is a specialty coffee shop based in Mexico City. Beyond serving great coffee, the project involves building and maintaining a comprehensive digital platform to power the business.

## Platform

The platform includes tools for point-of-sale operations, inventory management, staff performance tracking, customer analytics, and online ordering. It integrates with services like Stripe for payments, PostHog for product analytics, and various social media platforms for marketing and engagement.
